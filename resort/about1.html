HOMEPAGE ABOUT ENQUIRIES
One of the premier hotels in Mysuru, formerly known as Mysore, the Royal Palace Mysore offers city-center convenience and gorgeous views of the Chamundi Hills. If you’re arriving from Mandakalli Airport (MYQ), our hotel is just a 15-minute drive away. For some fun in the city, watch the antics of the animals at the Mysore Zoo or work on your short game at Jayachamaraja Wadiyar Golf Club, both located adjacent to the hotel. When you're ready to relax, unwind in one of our spacious rooms and suites, each with top-notch amenities like free Wi-Fi, 24-hour in-room dining, and our complimentary Super Breakfast buffet. Stay loyal to your exercise regimen by jogging or working out at our state-of-the-art fitness center, or swim a few laps in the crystal blue outdoor pool. Ideal for weddings, corporate events, and family gatherings, our event facilities can accommodate up to 1,200 guests and feature a 24-hour business center and the assistance of our talented events staff.

Lounge in our chic suites with views of the Chamundi Hills


Our well-appointed rooms and suites are furnished to the highest standards and feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen televisions with satellite channels, and comfortable work desks. Some include additional perks like a separate living room and a minibar. For a truly special experience, book one of our hill-view accommodations and indulge your senses with stunning views of the surrounding hillsides each morning as you slowly sip your first cup of tea or coffee.
